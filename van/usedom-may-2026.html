En-Route Stop

Kloster Chorin — Ruined Abbey

10 minutes off the A11 at junction 8 (Chorin). About 80 km from Falkenberg — roughly one hour into the drive. Worth 45–60 minutes.

⛪

Kloster Chorin · Schorfheide-Chorin Biosphere

Exit A11 junction 8 · Amt Chorin 11a, 16230 Chorin · €6 entry · open 9–6

A 13th-century Cistercian abbey — founded in 1258, dissolved in the Reformation, left to crumble, then rescued by the architect Karl Friedrich Schinkel in the 19th century. He called it one of "the state's most precious jewels." The west façade alone is worth the stop — proper Gothic architecture in brick, not a modest village church.

The ruins sit on a lake (Amtssee) inside a UNESCO biosphere reserve. You walk through roofless naves, vaulted cellars, and cloister remains with trees growing through the stonework. There are moulded-brick friezes with animal and vine motifs, 14th-century murals, and a warehouse cellar only uncovered a few years ago.

There's an organic café on the grounds — soups, homemade cake, coffee, ice cream, seating under big trees. Orla can walk the grounds with you.

At Karnin

The Lift Bridge Ruin

Right at your stellplatz doorstep — the reason Karnin is worth the stop.

🏗️The Karnin Lift Bridge (Hubbrücke Karnin) — a 35-metre steel skeleton standing in the middle of the Peenestrom. Built in 1933, it was the most modern lifting bridge in Europe: the railway line from Berlin to Swinemünde crossed here. Destroyed by retreating Wehrmacht troops on 28 April 1945. The ramps were blown up, but the central lift section survived — now a designated technical monument and bird colony.
📋Information boards at the harbour explain the full history. The bridge used the same engineering as the Niederfinow Boat Lift (still running). Its cables were pre-stressed at Niederfinow then shipped to Karnin by rail.
🔭The Lotsenturm (pilot tower) is nearby — the only one on Usedom, recalling the island's shipping history.
⛪Walk along the horse paddocks towards Mönchow (15 min) — a 15th-century late Gothic church and a mausoleum. Quiet, rural, Orla off-leash territory.
⛴️Small electric ferry crosses to Kamp on the mainland (May–Oct, hourly 10:00–17:00, ~€6). Nice to look at even if you don't cross.
