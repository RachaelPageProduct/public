Dual-brand overview

Artfuly and Rachael Page are two distinct brands owned by the same designer — Rachael Page. They share a typeface family, a navigation colour, and a teal accent. Everything else is intentionally differentiated: Artfuly is warm and educational; rachaelpage.com is crisp and portfolio-focused.

How they relate
artfuly.com
Design education platform
Resources, articles, courses. Warm teal palette. Coral CTAs. Merriweather for long-form reading.
rachaelpage.com
Professional portfolio
30 years UX/product work. Black and white dominant. Teal used as accent only. Montserrat throughout.
Same codebase, two domains. Both sites are built in Astro and deployed to Netlify. artfuly.com serves /; rachaelpage.com is served via a 200 rewrite at /rachael-page/. The shared teal nav colour (below) is the most visible design thread connecting them.

Artfuly logo

The Artfuly logo is a circle in mid-teal (#3ba89c) with the wordmark "Artfuly" in white Montserrat. The favicon is a teal square with a white "A" styled as an artist's easel. There is no separate standalone mark — the circle is the mark.

Logo on approved backgrounds
On white — primary use for marketing and print.
On light cyan — section backgrounds, email.
On dark teal — nav header, dark overlays.
Favicon
Favicon at 32px, 48px, 64px. Teal square, white A.
The favicon "A" is stylised as an artist's easel — a deliberate reference to art/design craft. It is not a generic letter A. Do not substitute with a plain A in any typeface.
Clear space
½ × logo diameter ½ × logo diameter ½ × dia. ½ × dia.
Minimum clear space on all sides = half the logo's diameter. No text, buttons, or imagery inside this zone.
Minimum sizes: Full logo (circle + wordmark together) — 80px diameter minimum. In the nav the logo is rendered at 32–36px. Below 24px, use only the favicon.
Don'ts
Artfuly
Don't change the circle colour. Only mid-teal (#3ba89c) is approved.
Artfuly
Don't stretch or distort the circle. It must remain a perfect circle.
Artfuly
Don't add drop shadows, glows, or effects to the logo.
Artfuly
Don't place on non-approved backgrounds. White, light cyan, and dark teal only.
Artfuly
Don't retype the wordmark in a different font. Always use the PNG asset.
Artfuly
Don't use the logo at reduced opacity.
