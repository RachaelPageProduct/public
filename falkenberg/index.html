Schweizerhaus Falkenhagen

€20–28 per dish
German cuisine Lakeside Terrace Lunch & dinner

A classic German inn right on the shores of Schwarzer See, with a cosy wood-panelled dining room, a glass conservatory, and a wooden deck stretching out over the lake. The menu features proper farm-sourced schnitzel, grilled beef rump steak, salmon fillet, and a handful of vegetarian options — solid cooking in a lovely setting. Eisgarten Buchholz

~€5 per dish
Café & diner Ice cream Homemade cakes Outdoor seating

The most beloved spot in Falkenhagen — a relaxed café and diner that punches well above its weight. Come for the legendary ice cream sundaes and extraordinary homemade cheesecake with meringue, stay for the surprisingly excellent rump steak. Friendly, attentive service and a lovely outdoor area make it a great choice for everything from a lazy afternoon coffee to a proper sit-down meal.

Strandidyll

€10–20 per dish
German cuisine Waterside Outdoor seating Takeaway

A hidden gem on the water's edge in Berkenbrück, with a large terrace and a relaxed, unhurried atmosphere. Classic German cooking — schnitzels, steaks, seasonal dishes — made with fresh local ingredients at very reasonable prices. Rated 4.9 out of 5 and well worth the short drive. Free parking, wine and beer, and takeaway available too.
